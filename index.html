A Message from the Principal | Mensaje del Director

Dear colleagues, this week brings with it a significant moment for our school: on Friday, DelCampo will have the honor of hosting the ABSH Math Olympics in collaboration with Academia Los Pinares. This is not just an event—it is a reflection of the excellence we represent and the trust our peers place in us as an institution.

Hosting an event of this magnitude requires more than logistical coordination. It requires a collective commitment to represent DelCampo at its very best. When schools from across our association arrive at our campus, they will not only see our facilities—they will experience our culture, our professionalism, and our spirit. Every interaction, every detail, every moment of hospitality speaks to who we are.

This week, I am asking for your flexibility, your collaboration, and your excellence. Wednesday and Thursday will be short days for students, followed by professional development time dedicated to preparing for this event. Friday, students will not be in session, but our team will be fully engaged in hosting. I know this asks a lot of you, and I am deeply grateful for your willingness to go the extra mile.

Hosting excellence is not just about what we do—it is about how we do it. Let us show our ABSH colleagues what DelCampo stands for: rigor, warmth, professionalism, and pride. Thank you for your partnership in making this week a success.
