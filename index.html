A Message from the Principal | Mensaje del Director

Welcome back, my High School team! I hope the vacation provided you with rest, renewal, and quality time with the people who matter most. As we return this week, we step into the final stretch of the school year—and what a meaningful stretch it is.

This Wednesday, we will hold our Student-Led Conferences. These conferences are powerful moments where students take ownership of their learning journey, share their progress with their families, and reflect on their growth and goals. Please prepare your students thoughtfully for these conversations. Guide them in presenting their work, celebrating their achievements, and discussing areas for continued growth. These conferences matter deeply.

As we move forward into this fourth quarter, I want to remind us all of the importance of maintaining our high standards in grading and evaluation practices right through to the end of the year. It is easy to lose focus as the finish line approaches, but our students deserve consistency, fairness, and rigor all the way through. This is especially critical as we think strategically about our seniors.

Our 12th graders will be in school only until May 5th. Their exams are scheduled for May 6th, 7th, and 8th. This means we have a very short window to evaluate them effectively and ensure they are prepared for those final assessments. We need to be intentional, focused, and strategic in how we use the remaining time with them. Every assignment, every class session, every piece of feedback counts.

Let's finish this year strong—not because we have to, but because our students deserve nothing less. Thank you for your professionalism, your care, and your unwavering commitment to excellence.
